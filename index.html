Location

Our office is strategically located in City Hall District. The building, Suntec Tower One, is part of the Suntec City integrated development and is a prominent landmark.


Getting to our office is convenient both by road and by MRT (Mass Rapid Transit) as the nearby Promenade MRT Station is accessible via the Circle and Downtown Lines.


From Changi Airport, it will take about 20 minutes via the East Coast Parkway (ECP) to our office which is located on the 37th storey overlooking the Marina Bay.
