Positions Available

We seek young and self-motivated Singaporeans/Permanent Residents to join our team for the positions below.

Audit Seniors/Associates

Reporting to the Managers, the successful candidates will be responsible for performing external audit for a portfolio of clients and other ad-hoc projects as assigned.


In addition, the ideal candidates should be able to complete audit assignments independently and be responsible for a team of junior staff for the Audit Senior position.


The ideal candidates should possess ACCA or recognized tertiary qualifications or equivalent with at least 2 years' experience in an international or reputable CPA firm for the Audit Senior position. Fresh graduates are welcomed to apply for the Audit Assistant position as training will be provided.

Accounts Associates

Reporting to the Manager, the successful candidates will be responsible for our clients' full set of accounts and payroll.


The ideal candidate should possess Diploma in Accountancy or equivalent with minimum 1-year experience. Knowledge of ACCPAC/MYOB Accounting is preferred. Candidates with Accounting degree with no experience are welcomed to apply as training will be provided.


An attractive remuneration package commensurate with qualifications and experience would be offered to the right candidate.

Tax Associates

Reporting to the Tax Partner, the successful candidates will be in charge of the tax advisory and compliance work for both individuals and corporations.


The ideal candidates should be CAs or possess a Degree in Business Studies with at least 3 years' relevant experience, preferably in an international public accounting practice. Ability to work under pressure and independently is a pre-requisite.

Admin Assistant

Reporting to Audit Partner, the successful candidate will be responsible for all admin/secretarial functions, which includes handling confidential correspondence and information.


In addition, she would be required to assist in the typing of audited accounts and related general correspondence for audit department as well as in answering phone calls and perform reception duties when necessary.


The ideal candidate should possess a Diploma with at least 1 year experience in a similar position. She should have a good command of English and be able to work independently with minimum supervision. Ability to speak Mandarin is an added advantage.

An attractive remuneration package commensurate with qualifications and experience will be given to the right candidate.

Please submit full resume with detailed qualifications, scanned copies of academic transcripts and certificate, testimonials, two (2) references, work experience, current and expected salary, recent photograph (n.r.) and contact details to careers@rohanmah.com

All applications will be treated with strict confidence.

Only shortlisted candidates will be notified.
