Our office is strategically in an iconic integrated development at the heart of the Marina Centre precinct of Suntec City which consist of 5 towers. We are located at Suntec Tower One.


A blend of architecture narrative with Fengshui, Suntec City is sculptured in the shape of a left hand. The five office towers represent the fingers and thumb while the palm cradles a bronze ring, the iconic Fountain of Wealth. This balance of metal with the flowing motion of water symbolises the preservation of wealth and success.


Getting to our office is convenient both by road and by MRT (Mass Rapid Transit) alight at Esplanade or Promenade^ via the Circle Line, and City Hall* via the East West Line.


From Changi Airport, it will take about 20 minutes via the East Coast Parkway (ECP) to our office which is located on the 37th level with a view of the Singapore skyline.

^Promenade is also on the Downtown Line.

*City Hall is on the East West Line and North South Line.
