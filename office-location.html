Office Location

Our office is strategically located right in the middle of Shenton Way - Singapore's Business District. The building, named AXA Tower, is currently the tallest cylindrical office building in the world and is a prominent landmark.


Getting to our office is convenient both by road and by MRT (Mass Rapid Transit) as Tanjong Pagar MRT Station is connected to the building basement.


From Changi Airport, it will take about 20 minutes via the East Coast Parkway (ECP) and Marina Coastal Expressway (MCE) to our office which is located on the 21st storey overlooking the seafront.
